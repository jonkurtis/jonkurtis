Like many other companies, our small marketing team was forced to work remotely in 2020 when the Covid-19 pandemic hit. As the head of a small marketing team that was used to working in close quarters, it was clear that Slack and individual Trello boards would no longer be sufficient to keep our team on track.

So I went searching for a project management tool that would allow my teammates to keep their Trello style Kanban workflows, while providing one centralized space for me to assign tasks, see the entire teams progress and streamline our workflow with automations.
